WHY WE EXIST

One Installation Inc is a low voltage wiring company with over 20 years experience in the industry. We offer various services, and take every step to assure the quality of our work is excellent. Our aim is to be your go-to installation company to meet all your home or business needs.

S E R V I C E S

STRUCTURED CABLING

We offer a wide range of structure cabling that could be designed to any of our customers needs. Our techs are ready to give you wire solutions for complex connections. One Install inc is state licensed and insured.

CCTV

We offer video security solutions for your home or business. Protect the people and property you care about by using state of the art digital technology and security.

WIRELESS NETWORK

We offer wireless communication and networking solutions, from basic connections to more complex networking projects.

AUDIO VISUAL

Whether it’s video conferencing, digital & touch displays, projectors, or distributed audio, we have the experience to meet all of your needs.

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

We offer around the clock IT support. Whether it's one of the above things we've installed in your home or business or you need a professional to troubleshoot or update your technology, we have you covered. We maintain our clients technology and infastruture and have on site support.
